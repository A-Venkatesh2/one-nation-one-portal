About One Nation One Portal

"One Nation One Portal" is a unified web platform built to provide easy access to all major official government websites of India — covering Central, State, and Union Territory administrations. This portal is part of an educational initiative to promote digital governance awareness and simplify access to government services.

Objectives

  • Centralized access to all government resources in one place.
  • Improve user experience for citizens and students seeking government portals.
  • Raise awareness of digital initiatives and online services.
  • Serve as an educational and reference project.

Key Features

  • Links to verified and official government portals.
  • Responsive design, mobile-friendly.
  • Real-time date and time display.
  • Simple, clean, and informative user interface.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • Bootstrap 5 for responsive layout
  • JavaScript for real-time clock
